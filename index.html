Projects

Most of my projects have been focused on making games.
Checkers

Built in C#, Had a lot of fun with this one. I used calculations of the board to tell it where player pieces were and what were legal and illegal moves. I also added in a mechanic where if multiple pieces could be jumped a timer would pop up so the player who jumped the first piece could then jump another if they made a move in time, if they could jump another piece after that the timer would reset and they can keep making moves over and over, as long as they were legal moves and within the time limit.

Dungeon explorer with rock paper sissors battle system

Made in java, was just a map made up of symbols like | and # depicting walls and paths respectively. the player was also a symbol that they could control and move around the map to gather items and run into enemys. the goal of the game was to find a key somewhere on the map and enter a boss room. the battles were very simple rock, paper, scissors decisions. items found could also effect battle.

Visual Novel with a battle system

Also made in java, this one was more fleshed out with images I made, which is why it will never see the light of day as long as I live. the concept of this game was to read through text prompts and make decisions throughout the story. depending on the choices it would change what the character was doing and how they developed. As the character developed they would unlock new skills during battle, earn money, buy armor/weapons, and recuit party members.

New World

Genres: Single Player, Fantasy, Action, JRPG

Description: An open world with a linear story and in an anime artstyle. Tainted Grail

Genres: Rougelike, Dark Fantasy, Turn Based, Deckbuilder
